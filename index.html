Summertime!

Summer is my favorite season!

By Om Patil
Intro to Software Technology
6/22/2023

I love the Summer! Here are some reasons as to why I love Summer so much:

  • Summer Break!: School is out, and I can finally relax!
  • Memorial Day: A day to honor Veterans who served our country
  • Juneteenth: Day to commemorate the emancipation of enslaved African Americans
  • Independence Day: July 4th- the USA gets independence from Great Britain
  • Father's Day: Day to honor our fathers
Since I don't have to go to school, I can spend time with my family, friends, and doing other things that I love, like playing sports like tennis or basketball or playing chess, coding, or learning new things!
